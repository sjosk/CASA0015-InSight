Privacy issue

Privacy Problem?

Our hardware part only contains a plr sensor to detect if someone is passing by, and you can rest assured that no camera will monitor your activity.

Device type support this apps?

Currently, we only support Android devices, and we will further expand to ios devices in the future, so stay tuned.

No hardware?

Of course, you can use our APP for free without purchasing our hardware. There's just no way to get an umbrella notification when you leave the house.

Price?

You can contact the author to purchase our hardware devices, each excluding postage costs £20, and our software is all open source and free to try.
